# Paradise Nursery - e-plantShopping

Welcome to the **e-plantShopping** application, a dedicated platform for houseplant enthusiasts. This project, titled **Paradise Nursery**, is a React-based web application designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for users looking to enhance their living spaces with greenery.

## 🌿 Project Overview
**e-plantShopping** is a functional e-commerce storefront where users can browse a curated selection of houseplants, group them by categories, and manage their purchases through a persistent shopping cart powered by Redux.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Landing Page:** An immersive welcome screen with a high-quality background and a "Get Started" call-to-action.
* **Product Selection:** Over 6 unique houseplant varieties categorized into "Air Purifying," "Aromatic," and "Medicinal" groups.
* **Redux Cart Management:** * Add items to the cart with a single click.
    * Buttons are dynamically disabled once an item is added.
    * Real-time cart icon badge showing the total item count.
* **Interactive Shopping Cart:** * Adjust quantities (increment/decrement) with instant price updates.
    * Delete items directly from the cart.
    * Automatic calculation of subtotals and grand totals.
* **Navigation:** A persistent navbar allowing users to toggle between the home landing page, the plant list, and the checkout area.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React.js
* **State Management:** Redux Toolkit
* **Styling:** CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid)
* **Icons/Assets:** FontAwesome & custom nursery imagery

## 📖 How to Use
1.  Click the **Get Started** button on the landing page to enter the store.
2.  Browse the plant categories and click **Add to Cart** on your favorites.
3.  Click the **Cart Icon** in the navigation bar to review your selections.
